What makes our apartments special?

Our seaside apartments, just 600m from the beach. Enjoy stunning views of the sea and Mount Olympus.

Nearby, find Beach Bars Kyma and Asterias, a supermarket, and a traditional Greek tavern.

We're also conveniently located for day trips: Potamos sea (15 mins), Thessaloniki Airport (25 mins), Cosmos Shopping Center (27 mins), and Thessaloniki city center (35 mins).

What makes our town special?

Epanomi, a charming town nestled along the northern coast of Greece, offers a delightful blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.

With its pristine beaches that stretch along the azure Aegean Sea, Epanomi is a haven for sun-seekers and water enthusiasts.

The town is also renowned for its vineyards, producing some of the finest wines in the region, making it a must-visit destination for wine connoisseurs.

Epanomi's picturesque streets are lined with quaint tavernas and cafes where you can savor authentic Greek cuisine while enjoying the warm hospitality of the locals.

Whether you're exploring its historical sites or simply basking in the Mediterranean sunshine, Epanomi promises an unforgettable experience for all who visit.
